2. Loan Eligibility

To qualify for a TNBF(Tamil Nadu Business Finance) business loan, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum Monthly Transactions: ₹15 Lakhs or more in business bank account
  • Active Business: Valid GST registration and business license
  • Current Account: Active business current account for minimum 6 months
  • Business Owner: Must be a business owner (not salaried employee)
  • Geographic Location: Business operating in Tamil Nadu or Bangalore
  • Age: Applicant must be 21 years or older

Important: Meeting eligibility criteria does not guarantee loan approval. TNBF(Tamil Nadu Business Finance) reserves the right to approve or reject applications at its sole discretion.

3. Loan Terms

Amount & Tenure

  • Loan Amount: ₹5 Lakhs to ₹10 Crores (up to 150% of monthly transactions)
  • Loan Tenure: 4 to 15 months
  • Interest Rate: Starting from 1.5% per month (subject to credit assessment)
  • Processing Fee: As applicable, disclosed upfront

Repayment

  • Repayment through post-dated cheques (PDCs)
  • Monthly installment schedule shared during agreement
  • No prepayment penalties
  • Late payment charges as per agreement

4. Application Process

The loan application process involves:

  1. Submission of online application with required documents
  2. Verification of documents and business transactions (24-48 hours)
  3. Credit assessment and eligibility evaluation
  4. Loan approval or rejection notification
  5. Agreement signing and PDC submission
  6. Fund disbursement to business account

5. Documentation Requirements

Applicants must provide:

  • Aadhar Card and PAN Card
  • GST Registration Certificate
  • Business registration proof
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Business address proof
  • Post-dated cheques as per repayment schedule
